Getting Started with Dialogflow

Dialogflow is Google's natural language understanding tool for building conversational experiences, such as voice apps and chatbots, powered by AI. Connect with users on your website, mobile app, the Google Assistant, Amazon Alexa, Facebook Messenger, and other popular platforms and devices.

Building a conversational event Scheduler chatbot

Let's take a look at a sample

Try typing "schedule event", it will ask you to specify the day and finally time.

Before we dive in , lets try defining some few terms "ll encounter.

  1. Agent:

    2. Is a virtual agent that handles conversations with your end-users. It is a natural language understanding module that understands the nuances of human language. It handle the types of conversations required for your system.

  2. Intent:

    3. Categorizes end-user intentions for each conversation turn.

  3. Entity:

    4. Helps to identify and extract specific data from end-user expressions.

  4. Integration:

    5. Applications that run on devices or services that directly handle end-user interactions for you (for example, Google Assistant).

  • 1 First step

    Ensure you have your google mail. Login to dialogflow dashboad. Take a look at this link and Go to console. Located on top-right corner Dialogflow.

  • 2 Second step

    Create new Agent. Give it a name.

    An agent is the name of the chatbot. For this test we name "googleiokisii". Click SAVE.

    In your dashboard you will see , a Default Welcome intent and Default Fallback intent.

    Default Fallback Intent:

    This intent is matched when your user's input does not match any other intent; in other words, it's a kind of catch-all for any unrecognized user input.

    Default Welcome Intent:

    Has a special WELCOME event which is triggered whenever the user begins a conversation with your agent via supported one-click integrations.

  • 3 Third step

    Create new Intent that will override default intents.

    In this project we have created an intent called "eventSchedule". create yours.. Click save.

    In your entity, give your bot some training phrases i.e event schedule event at 3pm, event on Monday

    Phrases you can expect from users, that will trigger the intent.
  • 4 Fourth step

    Adding Actions And Parameters

    They 're used to trigger questions for this case in our project questions like . When is the event?, specify time? are captured here.

    Create yours

    Give out appropriate response basing on your training phrases and actions and parameters.

  • 5 Fifth step

    Integration

    The question is . How will users interract with my chatbot? Let's take a look. first navigate to Integration and click Web dem to enable. Copy the embed link provided and paste it in your website.
    Congratulations you have just created your first conversational chatbot. Dive in now to create a more complex one! Nice chatting
